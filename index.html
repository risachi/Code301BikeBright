Your bike safe

What we are

More commuters bike to work in Portland than in any other major U.S. city, and bicycle theft is on the rise. Our goal is to help you figure out where to park your bicycle by showing you where bicycles get stolen most often - so you can avoid parking in those areas. Bike more, worry less.
